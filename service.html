Our Services

From general dentistry wellness,to perfecting cosmetic issues, to implants and restorations, to the painful dental emergencies- we've got you covered for all your dental related problems.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Creating a naturally beautiful smile requires a keen observation and attention to the small minor details. Cosmetic dentistry includes composite and porcelain veneers, teeth whitening and smile makeovers. By using the best quality dental material and by partnering with the best dental labs, it has enabled us to transform smiles beautifully and naturally for you.

Dental Implants

Implants are a modern solution to replace missing teeth, they can be used to replace a single tooth, multiple teeth or all of the teeth. Implants are the best way to replace the missing teeth as they are natural looking, stimulate the bone and hence prevent bone loss at the site of the missing teeth and are long lasting.

Root Canals & Endodontics

Save your natural teeth and eliminate the pain and discomfort. Advanced decay involving the tooth pulp(nerves) requires the need of a root canal treatment. At Dentrust, we've made this horrifing procedure totally painless and comfortable for you. With the use of advance equipments and technologies, root canals are now faster, comfortable and more successful.

Dentures

Replace the missing teeth by the help of dentures.They can be both fixed and removable, fixed dentures are possible by implants. Dentures depending upon the need can be partial or complete. With the use of top class materials they help in restoring your ability to chew, speak and smile making you more confident and boosting your self esteem.

Orthodontics (Braces)

A great looking smile can boost your self confidence and have a positive impact on your social and professional life. There are a number of options to choose from when opting for orthodontic treatment like metal braces, ceramic braces, lingual braces and Invisalign. With the correct diagnosis and treatment plan you can achieve a well aligned brighter smile.

Child Dental Care

Parents have big expectations when entrusting their child to a dentist hence we work hard to cultivate an environment that is friendly, nurturing and fun and are committed to always treating our patients and their families with compassionate services and empathy. Along with treating the child, we also educate the child about healthy oral practices.

Surgical Procedure

While it may never be your or even your dentist's first choice to remove a tooth, sometimes it is inevitable to extract a tooth in order to restore the function and overall health. Extraction of wisdom tooth, implant placements and surgical removal of any abscess or pathology are all part of the surgical management along with post treatment instructions.

General Dentistry Procedures

Our approach to general dentistry starts with patient education. We'll provide you with the best information about oral health aiding in development of good at-home habits to maintain it. Routine dental checkups every 6 months help in early diagnosis and treatment. Use of latest technologies like low radiation x-rays & sensors, intraoral camera, robotic endodontics and much more, we aim to change your perception about dentistry.
