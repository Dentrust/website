Our Services

From general dentistry wellness,to perfecting cosmetic issues, to implants and restorations, to the painful dental emergencies- we've got you covered for all your dental related problems.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Dental Implants

Implants are a modern solution to replace missing teeth, they can be used to replace a single tooth, multiple teeth or all of the teeth. Implants are the best way to replace the missing teeth as they are natural looking, stimulate the bone and hence prevent bone loss at the site of the missing teeth and are long lasting. Implants can support single tooth crowns, bridges and even complete dentures which are called overdentures.

Root Canals & Endodontics

Save your natural teeth and eliminate the pain and discomfort. while mild tooth decay can be corrected by fillings and restorations, more advanced decay involving the tooth pulp(nerves) requires the need of a root canal treatment. The signs which might suggest the need of a root canal treatment include severe tooth senstivity, painful and constant tooth ache, hollow tooth, discolouration or darkening of the tooth, soreness in the bone related to the tooth, recently fractuerd tooth, any gingival swelling or a gingival pimple with fluid inside or oozing out. At Dentrust, we've made this horrifing procedure totally painless and comfortable for you. With the use of advance equipments and technologies, root canals are now faster, comfortable and more successful.

Dentures

Replace the missing teeth by the help of dentures.They can be both fixed and removable, fixed dentures are possible by implants. Dentures depending upon the need can be partial or complete. With the use of top class materials they help in restoring your ability to chew, speak and smile making you more confident and boosting your self esteem.

Orthodontics (Braces)

A great looking smile can boodt your self confidence and have a positive impact on your social and professional life. Whether you are considering straightening your teeth for cosmetic or health related reasons, both are equally important. There are a number of options to choose from when opting for orthodontic treatment like metal braces, ceramic braces, lingual braces and Invisalign. With the correct diagnosis and treatment plan you can achieve a well aligned brighter smile.

Child Dental Care

Parents have big expectations when entrusting their child to a dentist hence we work hard to cultivate an environment that is friendly, nurturing and fun and are committed to always treating our patients and their families with compassionate services and empathy.

Surgical Procedure

While it may never be your or even your dentist's first choice to remove a tooth, sometimes it is inevitable to extract a tooth in order to restore the function and overall health. Extraction becomes the only option when the damage or the deacy to the tooth is beyond repair, or severe bone has occured do to a long standing infection making the tooth and the bone vulnerable, or the wisdom tooth is causing trouble in eruption leading to cheek biting, trauma to the anterior tooth and infection. All these situations lead to extractions. We have attempted to make extractions painless so that you dont feel a thing while we are operating and our special list of post extraction instructions and tips aims to help you feel at ease even after you go home and are healing.

General Dentistry Procedures

Our approach to general dentistry starts with patient education. We'll provide you with the best information about teeth and oral health which will help you in developing good at-home habits and help you in maintaining good oral health. Routine dental checkups every 6 months help in early diagnosis and treatment. Along with the use of latest technologies like low radiation x-rays and digital sensors, intraoral camera, robotic endodontics and much more, we aim to change your perception about dentistry.
